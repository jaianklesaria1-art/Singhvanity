A Pioneer in Every Sense

Since 2009, Singh Vanity has been a pioneer in the Indian vanity van industry — proudly owning and operating the first-ever vanity van in the Bollywood film industry. What began as a breakthrough innovation has grown into a legacy of excellence, setting benchmarks in luxury, comfort, and on-location lifestyle solutions. From the very beginning, our vision has been simple yet powerful — to bring a home-like experience on wheels, elevated with elite design, functionality, and sophistication.

Over the years, our focus has been deeply rooted in understanding the evolving and intricate needs of the entertainment industry. Every vanity van we create is not just a vehicle, but a carefully crafted luxury environment designed to deliver an exclusive, premium experience. We believe that true luxury lies in detail, and every space we design reflects precision, comfort, and purpose.

"I have always believed in the power of persistence over noise. Success is not universal — it is personal, evolving differently for every individual and every journey."

While we have grown with the industry, our philosophy remains grounded. At Singh Vanity, this belief has shaped our foundation, allowing us to build more than a business; we have built a family-driven culture where trust, warmth, and dedication define every creation.

Built for the Next Generation

From the early days of simple requirements to today's highly advanced and creative demands, we have continuously evolved with the industry. As a new generation of artists, creators, and performers brings more imagination and diversity, we are inspired to design vanity vans that match every personality, style, and vision. Our goal is to blend luxury with emotion, structure with comfort, and innovation with familiarity.

Today, Singh Vanity stands as a symbol of trust, legacy, and innovation — delivering vanity vans that do not just serve a purpose, but create an experience. A space where luxury feels personal, comfort feels natural, and every journey feels like a moment of fame.
